## 4. The Problem

Hiring still feels heavy because the first mile is messy and repetitive.

- Role requirements are vague, so founders and HR spend days clarifying responsibilities, must-have skills, seniority, compensation, and team fit.
- One open role can receive 200+ resumes, but only a small fraction are worth advancing.
- Scheduling first-round interviews creates delays, especially across time zones.
- Different interviewers ask different questions, making candidate comparisons inconsistent.
- Startups often lack a dedicated recruiter or trained interview panel.
- Global hiring requires language coverage and always-on availability that most teams cannot staff.

**Result:** Teams move slowly, lose strong candidates, and spend too much time on low-signal work.

## 6. What Makes RoboHire Different

### Not another ATS
Most recruiting software stores information. RoboHire moves the work forward by clarifying, screening, interviewing, and preparing the scorecard.

RoboHire is not just keyword matching. It reads resumes and interview answers semantically, connects related experience, identifies gaps, and surfaces reasoning.

### Consistent evaluation
Every candidate can be evaluated against the same structured rubric, which makes comparisons cleaner and reduces arbitrary differences between interviewers.

## 8. Typical Startup Use Case

**Scenario:** A founder-led team needs to hire a senior product manager.

**Monday morning:** The founder tells the AI recruiting consultant what they need. RoboHire clarifies scope, must-have skills, seniority, compensation, and team fit. It generates the job description and helps launch the role.

**Monday before lunch:** The team uploads 150 resumes. RoboHire ranks the top candidates with clear reasoning and highlights gaps and strengths.

**Monday afternoon:** RoboHire automatically invites the best-fit candidates to complete first-round AI interviews.

**Wednesday:** Candidates have completed interviews on their own schedules. The team receives structured scorecards and meets only the final 3 to 4 finalists.

**Outcome:** The team spends its time on high-signal finalists instead of manual triage.

## 15. FAQ

**Is RoboHire only for HR teams?**  
No. It is useful for founders, hiring managers, HR teams, recruiting agencies, RPO teams, and startup program partners.

**Does RoboHire replace human hiring decisions?**  
No. RoboHire handles repetitive first-round work and creates structured reports. Humans still make final interview and hiring decisions.

**Can candidates interview outside business hours?**  
Yes. AI interviews can run 24/7.

**Which languages are supported for interviews?**  
English, Chinese, Japanese, Spanish, French, Portuguese, and German.

**Can RoboHire integrate with our existing systems?**  
Yes. RoboHire supports APIs, webhooks, and ATS integrations such as Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, BambooHR, and Workable.

**Can we use it for high-volume hiring?**  
Yes. The workflow is especially useful for roles with many applicants, campus hiring, distributed hiring, and teams with limited recruiter bandwidth.

**Can we start without a long implementation?**  
Yes. The product is designed to launch quickly, and a 14-day free trial is available.
